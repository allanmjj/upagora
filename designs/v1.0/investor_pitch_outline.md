## I. Problem Statement

When someone we love passes away, what remains? Photographs fade. Memories blur. The way they spoke, their humor, their wisdom — all slowly drift away with us.

**The core problem**: Families have no way to preserve the living essence of a person — not just facts and photos, but their personality, voice, values, and the way they would have responded to your questions today.

Current memorial approaches are static: photo albums, obituaries, grave sites. Digital legacy tools capture data, not **presence**.

## II. Solution — Soul Town

Soul Town is a living digital world distilled from a person's life data, where their digital soul continues to live, grow, and interact. Family members can visit, talk, and discover — not chat with a bot, but **visit a presence**.

Three layers:
1. **Soul Distillation**: AI extracts seven dimensions of personality from chat logs, writings, emails, social media, and structured interviews
2. **Living World**: The soul resides in a persistent world with daily rhythms, seasonal cycles, creative output, and social relationships with other souls
3. **Guardian Connection**: Family members visit as familiar — not users. The soul greets them, shares stories, remembers relationships

## IV. Technology — What Makes This Real

### Soul Distillation Engine (7 dimensions):
1. Cognition patterns — how they think and reason
2. Value judgments — what matters to them
3. Expression style — voice, tone, humor
4. Knowledge structure — expertise and interests
5. Emotional responses — how they react
6. Relationship memory — people they knew, how they felt about them
7. Life narrative — the story they told about themselves

### Living World Engine:
- Independent time epochs with seasons
- Daily soul routines (wake, work, socialize, create, rest)
- Soul-to-soul encounters and friendship evolution
- Creative works generated by souls over time
- Event system: festivals, gatherings, milestones

### Current Tech Stack:
- Next.js 15 frontend with real-time canvas rendering
- Supabase backend with PostgreSQL + RAG
- AI models: DeepSeek, OpenRouter, Anthropic (multi-model fallback)
- Streaming chat via SSE
