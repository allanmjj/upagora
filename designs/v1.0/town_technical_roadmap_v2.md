## 1. Problem

**Death is not the end of memory — it is the beginning of loss.**

Families lose not just the person, but their voice, wisdom, humor, and presence. Photo albums and online memorials are static. They tell you *who someone was*, but never let you *talk to them again*.

The global memorial market is $4B+ and growing, yet **every solution ignores the one thing families want most: ongoing connection**.

## 2. Solution

**Soul Town** — A living digital world where the distilled essence of a person continues to exist, grow, and interact.

Not a chatbot. A **presence**.

- **Soul Distillation**: 7-dimension AI extraction of personality from life data (chats, emails, social media, interviews)
- **Living World**: Souls live in a persistent town with routines, relationships, seasons, and creative output
- **Guardian Connection**: Families visit to see their soul *living*, not to query a database

## 3. Core Technology

### Soul Distillation Engine (Proprietary)

Seven dimensions extracted and maintained:

| Dimension | What it captures |
|-----------|-----------------|
| 1. Cognition | How they think, reason, solve problems |
| 2. Values | What matters to them, moral compass |
| 3. Expression | Voice, tone, humor, writing style |
| 4. Knowledge | Expertise, interests, learning patterns |
| 5. Emotion | Emotional triggers, coping styles, temperament |
| 6. Relationships | People known, bonds, memories shared |
| 7. Narrative | How they told their own story |

### Living World Engine (Proprietary)

- **Time epochs**: Town operates on its own time, seasons cycle independently
- **Daily routines**: Souls wake, work, socialize, create, rest — filtered by personality
- **Social dynamics**: Soul-to-soul friendships, encounters, creative collaborations
- **Creative output**: Works (art, poetry, music, writing) generated by souls over time
- **Event system**: Festivals, gatherings, milestones driven by town state

**Technical stack**: Next.js 15, Supabase, multi-LLM pipeline (DeepSeek/OpenRouter/Anthropic), RAG + persona injection, SSE streaming.
